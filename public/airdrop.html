🐔 CLUCK'S AIRDROPPER 101
1. WHAT IT DOES. You give it a list of Solana wallet addresses + amounts. It builds Solana transactions that transfer your tokens to all of them, batched ~10 wallets per transaction to stay under Solana's 1232-byte tx limit.
2. YOU SIGN EVERY BATCH. Your wallet pops up once per transaction. You see the exact instructions before approving. Reject if anything looks off.
3. RENT FOR NEW ACCOUNTS. If a recipient doesn't already hold this token, Solana requires ~0.00204 SOL to open a new token account for them. You pay this, not the recipient. The tool shows you the total up-front in the preview.
4. IRREVERSIBLE. Solana transactions can't be undone. Wrong address = lost tokens. Verify your recipient list twice before clicking SEND.
5. CLKN PAYS FOR ACCESS, NOT YOUR AIRDROP. The 100 CLKN you send unlocks the tool for an hour. Your airdrop tokens are separate — they leave your wallet only when YOU sign the batches in your own wallet (11 supported).
6. THINGS THAT GO WRONG. Not enough SOL for rent. Typo in a recipient address. Sending the wrong amount because you read the column wrong. Preview before you sign every time.
