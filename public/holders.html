📜 HOW TO READ THIS LIST

Holder counts and balances are facts from the chain. Categories (HUMAN / LP / LOCKED / PROGRAM) are derived from on-chain account ownership — but a wallet's role (team, supporter, sniper, holder) isn't on-chain.

Verified-creator badges (💸 BAGS CREATOR) appear only when confirmed by the Bags API, and on the autopsy page that confirmation is cross-checked against Solana Tracker's independent indexer. Treat everything else as data, not judgement.
