🧱 Cluck Order Book

See where buyers and sellers are waiting around any Solana token — resting limit orders, single‑sided walls, and how much it takes to move the price. The chain shows what's there, never why.

Try: CLKN WIF JUP
❓ How to read this — plain English
Limit order / wall — a buy or sell parked at a set price, waiting. A big sell wall above = resistance; a big buy wall below = support.
Depth chart — the green (buy) and red (sell) curves show the cumulative money stacked up as you move away from the current price. Tall, close‑in walls move price; a flat curve means little is resting.
Liquidity depth / price impact — how far the price moves when someone trades a size, across every pool at once. A $5k buy moving +30% = thin; barely moving = deep.
Single‑sided Orca position — a one‑sided liquidity position acts like a limit order. Add your LP wallet(s) above to tag ✓ yours vs ⚠ 3rd‑party.
