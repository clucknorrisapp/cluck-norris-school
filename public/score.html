📜 HOW TO READ THIS SCORE

A Cluck Score reports measurable health signals from the chain — not a verdict on the team or token. Liquidity, holders, authorities, and concentration are facts; what they mean for the future of the project depends on intent and execution we cannot see.

Use this as a research guide, not a buy / sell signal. We label what we can verify (Bags/Pump-registered creators, Jupiter audit data, Solana Tracker identity tags on the autopsy page) and describe everything else neutrally. Tokens scoring high can still rug; tokens scoring low can still be early. Always do your own research.
