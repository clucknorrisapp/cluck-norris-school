WHAT IS BAGS.FM?

Bags.fm is a Solana-native token launch platform. New tokens mint into a bonding curve (DBC) where early supporters buy directly from the curve at algorithmically-priced points. Once the curve hits its graduation threshold, the token migrates to a public liquidity pool (DAMM v2) and trades like any other Solana token from that point forward.

What sets Bags apart from older launchers: project teams register their wallet and social handles on-chain through Bags, with explicit royalty shares for each verified creator. That's real, public accountability baked right into the launch — and creators keep earning from their token's success for as long as it trades.

WHAT MAKES BAGS.FM DIFFERENT
💎 VERIFIED, ON-CHAIN CREATORS
Teams register their wallet and socials on-chain through Bags, with explicit royalty shares. You can see who's behind a token and how the fees are split — transparency most launchpads simply don't offer.
💸 CREATORS EARN FOREVER
Every trade routes a small creator royalty — paid in SOL — to the registered team, even after graduation. It aligns creators with the long-term success of their token instead of a one-time launch payday.
🔄 SUSTAINABLE BUY-AND-LOCK
Bags's fee structure lets healthy teams — CLKN included — recycle their SOL royalties into buying back their own token and locking the supply, permanently removing it from circulation. A genuinely sustainable model for a community token.
🎓 REAL GRADUATION
When a token clears its bonding curve it graduates to a live Meteora DAMM pool and trades like any other Solana token — real liquidity, not a fake "fair launch" narrative.
