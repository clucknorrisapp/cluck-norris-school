A free, open-source crypto school and public token-health toolkit built on Solana — teaching first-time users how to survive on-chain, and giving anyone a free way to vet any Solana token. No signup. No wallet connect required to learn.

WHAT'S LIVE AND FREE TODAY

Everything below is deployed in production at clucknorris.app, open source under the MIT license, and free to use by anyone — not gated behind the project's token.

FREE PUBLIC GOOD

🩻 Wallet X-Ray — full-wallet deep dive for any address

Paste any Solana wallet, get its whole story in seconds: the true funding origin traced all the way to the wallet's first transaction (with dust/spam filtered out and exchange hot-wallets identified), every buy, sell and transfer across every token, and a behavior read — bot cadence, fast-flip dumping, CEX cash-out, LP, or diamond-hands holder. A searchable timeline, charts, and a built-in "ask Cluck Norris about this transaction" AI. Served free at /wallet-xray. Helps any user — not just ours — vet a wallet before trusting it.

FREE PUBLIC GOOD

🔬 Token Autopsy — free forensic post-mortem for any mint

Served free at /autopsy: paste any Solana mint and an agent runs ~10 on-chain forensic phases — LP lock/burn status, a Token-2022 honeypot-extension scan, top-100 holder classification, locked-supply measurement, real-creator detection (not the launchpad's shared genesis wallet), creator buy-back and fee-reinvestment tracing, and hidden-exit (transfer-then-dump) detection — cross-verified against Bags, Jupiter and Solana Tracker, then explained in plain English. It deliberately labels only what it can prove and describes everything else neutrally. A genuine public good: it helps any Solana user understand a token before they buy, and teaches them what the warning signs look like.

FREE PUBLIC GOOD

🎓 The School — crypto safety curriculum

A structured, free curriculum for total beginners: wallets, tokens, DEXs, liquidity, market cap, and how not to get drained. A "Where do I start?" concierge meets newcomers on arrival — they say where they are on their crypto journey and get routed to the right lesson or tool, so a feature-rich app never overwhelms a first-timer. Includes a Survival Simulator that walks new users through real-world crypto scenarios — a bank of 135, with 10 drawn at random per run — using simulated capital, so they practice the dangerous decisions before making them with real money. No signup, no wallet, no paywall. Passing the server-scored Ultimate Challenge (the answer key never reaches the browser) — or finishing the full curriculum — mints a permanent, verifiable transcript keyed to the learner's wallet, so the achievement is real proof, not a clearable browser badge.

FREE PUBLIC GOOD

🛡 Wallet Safety Checkup — catch what actually drains people

A free, read-only scan at /wallet-checkup — paste any address, no wallet connect. One report on the real first-weeks killers: lingering token approvals (the one permission that persists on Solana), honeypot / can't-sell holdings, and tokens whose dev can still freeze or mint. It routes each finding to revoke it or research it. Most newcomer losses aren't bad token picks — they're forgotten approvals and drainer bait; this targets exactly that.

FREE PUBLIC GOOD

📣 Cluck's Lesson + Ask Cluck — learning inside the chat

Short crypto-safety lessons post automatically to the community Telegram (and X) several times a day. Members can reply to any lesson and an AI tutor answers right in-chat, with threaded follow-ups so it becomes a real back-and-forth — open to everyone, free, 20 questions/user/day. It stays strictly educational: it declines buy/sell/price and off-topic asks and ends every answer with a not-financial-advice line. New members are greeted with a "Where do I start?" concierge — tap-through journey buttons or just reply, and the same app-aware tutor points them to the right lesson or tool. This meets newcomers where they already are instead of asking them to go find a course.

FREE PUBLIC GOOD

📸 Snapshot — holder snapshots for any token

A free, token-agnostic holder snapshot tool at /snapshot. Paste any mint, get every wallet and balance, with LP pools and lock contracts filtered out by default — the #1 mistake new projects make when running airdrops. Education panel built in.

REUSABLE PRIMITIVE

🔓 No-wallet-connect micropayment pattern

An open-source pattern that lets a web app verify an on-chain SPL payment without a wallet-connect, signature request, or custody — the app generates a unique decimal amount, the user sends it from any wallet, and the server matches the transaction on-chain. Any Solana builder can fork this approach from our MIT repo. It lowers the friction and the risk surface for paid features across the ecosystem.

WHY THIS BENEFITS SOLANA
  • Reduces newcomer losses. Most new users on Solana lose money to rugs, drains, and bad tokens in their first weeks. Cluck Norris teaches them before they get hurt — and the free Token Autopsy and Wallet X-Ray give them a way to vet a token or wallet before trusting it.
  • Free and token-agnostic. The Token Autopsy, Wallet X-Ray, the school, and the snapshot tool work for any Solana token and any user. They're public goods, not funnels for one project.
  • Open source. MIT-licensed. Other builders can fork the curriculum, the forensic engine, or the no-wallet-connect payment pattern.
  • Reaching Solana Mobile. The app is wrapped as a native Android app (Capacitor) and is live on the Solana Seeker dApp Store — putting free crypto-safety education directly on the Seeker. Deeper Mobile Wallet Adapter / Seed Vault integration is on the roadmap; today the app delivers the full web experience natively, not in-app native wallet signing.
  • Already shipped. This is not a roadmap — it's a working product in production. The grant scales what's proven, with low execution risk.
